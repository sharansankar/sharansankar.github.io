PROJECTS

DISCOVERY BOT

ROBOHACKS 2016

Arduino

Oculus

VR

Myo

The third place finisher, Discovery Bot was our idea of making space exploration a more organic and human-like process. We wanted users to feel like exploring another planet could be as simple as looking around and pointing. To do this we incorporated a rover, a Myo arm band to control the rover, and a VR Environment of the mars terrain.

Our project incorporated three main components:

Arduino: we built our rover using the Arduino microcontroller to perform just like a rover with 4 independent motors and a movable claw.

Myo: to make interactions of the rover and its environment more natural to the user, we incorporated a myo band with our rover using MyoDuino libraries.

Occulus: to create an environment to simulate our rover's functionality, we created a mars virtual environment using the Occulus VR libraries for the Unity Graphics engine. The environment was made from scratch using this library.
